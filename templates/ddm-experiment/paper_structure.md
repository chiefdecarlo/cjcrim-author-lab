# Paper Structure: DDM Experiment

## Sections

1. Title Page
2. Abstract
3. Introduction (mechanism focus)
4. Theoretical Framework (sequential sampling models)
5. Method
   - Participants
   - Design
   - Stimuli
   - Procedure
   - Apparatus (eye tracking, physiology if applicable)
   - Analysis plan
6. Results
   - Behavioral
   - DDM parameters
   - Eye tracking (if applicable)
   - Mediation/Moderation
7. Discussion
8. Limitations
9. Conclusion
10. References

## Required Tables
- Participant demographics
- Behavioral summary (RT, accuracy by condition)
- DDM parameter estimates
- Model comparison

## Required Figures
- RT distributions by condition
- DDM parameter posteriors
- Gaze patterns (if eye tracking)
